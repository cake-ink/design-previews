The other platforms take a cut of every booking and charge your clients a fee on top. Artists told us exactly what they think of that. We built the opposite.

No cut of your bookings. Ever.

Not 10 percent, not 1 percent. Your money moves straight from your client to you.

No chatbots. Ever.

Automations can send the messages you approve, but your clients always reach a human: you.

Your name is yours, free.
